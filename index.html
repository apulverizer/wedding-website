About Us

Aaron

I was born and raised in Naples, NY – a small town tucked away in the middle of the beautiful finger lakes. I received my Bachelor’s degree in computer engineering from RIT in 2014, and then I took a five-month long casual walk from Maine to Georgia. After I finished with the Appalachian Trail, Dawn and I moved to Salt Lake City, so I could obtain my Master’s degree in Geography from the University of Utah. In my free time I enjoy hiking, biking, watching the Bills lose, being a nerd, and pwning n00bs.

Dawn

I’m from a small town outside of Utica, NY (You’ll remember that name when you taste the food at the wedding). I attended college at RIT where I met Aaron, and finished my undergraduate education at SUNY Brockport. I currently reside in Salt Lake City, Utah but I’m eagerly awaiting our move to Maine. I’m obsessed with our rescue dog, Dacks. I enjoy the outdoors, not wearing pants, listening to sad white girl music, yelling at video games, spending time with my homies, and general cheeky shenanigans.
